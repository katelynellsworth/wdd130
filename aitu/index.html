Prices/Dates

Here we pride ourselves in giving people great trips they will remember for years to come. The groups are made for 10 people with guides and security. This will make sure you get the attention and care as we discover the beauty all round. We go away from tourist destinations and see places only locals know about. Make sure you being a camera with plenty memory as your experiences are sure to blow others away. We will be making are way through places hotels don’t exist but don’t fret we have cottages with all the amenities along the way providing great places to sleep and food to eat during your 3-week trip. Leaving the well-known trail requires some extreme sports to get to where we are going. Schedule you training meeting so we can make sure you are ready for the amazing trip you are going to have. We look forward to experiencing this trip with you so get ready and sign up.

Extreme Sports

Come Experience Beauty And Thrill

Enjoy the sports you love with a breathtaking scenery. From valleys, waterfalls, canyons, and mountains. The sport we will be enjoying are rock climbing, horseback riding, ATV Riding, snow mobile riding, river rafting, ice climbing, skiing or snowboarding, ice caving, dog sledding, scuba diving, canoeing / kayaking, fishing, surfing, mountain biking, sky diving and paragliding. Certification will be needed in advance before going on the trip as not having the right certification will make you ineligible to participate. We hope to experience our next trip with you!
